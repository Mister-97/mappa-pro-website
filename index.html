Your monthly fee per creator is based on that creator's gross earnings for the month. The more they earn, the higher the tier — but all features are included at every tier. You only pay more when your creators earn more.
Yes. Mappa Pro connects to Fanvue through their official OAuth 2.0 integration. We never store passwords or credentials — creator accounts are authorized with a single click and tokens are AES-256 encrypted and auto-refreshed.
There's no limit on the number of creators or team members. You're billed per creator profile, not per seat — so add as many chatters and managers as your agency needs at no extra cost.
You're invoiced on the 1st of each month for the previous month's usage, with payment due by the 5th. Billing is calculated per creator based on their gross earnings for that period.
