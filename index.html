Features

Everything your agency needs in one place

Purpose-built for Fanvue agencies managing multiple creator profiles and teams of chatters.

Unified Inbox
Manage conversations from all your Fanvue accounts in one place. Never miss a message, flag follow-ups, and let chatters handle fans efficiently.
Revenue Analytics
Track earnings, subscriptions, tips, PPV revenue and top spenders across every creator account — today, this month, or all time.
Fan Insights
See exactly how much each fan has spent, their subscription history, spending behavior, and personal notes — right from the inbox.
Scripts & Snippets
Build conversation scripts for your chatters and quick-reply snippets with variable substitution. Personalize at scale without extra effort.
Team Management
Add owners, managers, and chatters with role-based access. Assign chatters to specific creator accounts and track performance by team member.
Secure OAuth Connection
Connect Fanvue accounts via official OAuth — no passwords stored. Tokens are AES-256 encrypted and automatically refreshed.
